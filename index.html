Our Vision & Mission

OUR VISION

Building women with godly characters, to equip them to be fully functional in their various God-given assignments.

Our Core Values

Full Dependence on God

We rely completely on God's guidance, wisdom, and provision in all we do.

Displaying a Godly Character

We strive to reflect Christ's character in our thoughts, words, and actions.

A Healed Vessel

We believe in healing and restoration to become effective vessels for God's use.

Holiness

We pursue holiness in all areas of life as an act of worship to God.

Sense of Community

We foster genuine relationships and support one another in our spiritual journeys.

Our Founder

Leading Women to God's Purpose

The founder of Morning Devotion is a woman dedicated to building women of godly character through prayer, fasting, and the Word of God. With a heart for healing and restoration, she has created a safe space for women to grow spiritually and support one another in their journeys.

Inspired by Psalm 5:3, she emphasizes the importance of morning devotion and seeking God's guidance at the start of each day. Her vision has grown into a community where women are equipped to be fully functional in their various God-given assignments.

"Every morning, I lay out the pieces of my life on the altar and wait for your fire to fall upon my heart. This is the heart of Morning Devotion."
